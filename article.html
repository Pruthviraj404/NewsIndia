Global Markets Rally as Inflation Data Eases

By Rajesh Kumar Published on Feb 15, 2026 • 5 min read

Equities climbed today as investors digested fresh economic data and positive corporate earnings. Markets were driven by optimism around growth and easing concerns about inflation.

Major indices finished the session higher after a volatile morning, with technology and consumer sectors leading the gains. Analysts note that momentum could continue if incoming data remains supportive.
